Press Release
The aptly named Team Gravedigger is proud to announce that its debut title Gravedigger will be released on May 8th, 2015 on PC, Mac, and Linux platforms.

From a young age, Barry Manbelow always loved digging and this addiction has made it hard to hold down a steady job. As a life-guard, he was always more concerned with burying kids in the sand than saving them from drowning. As a dog sitter, he helped man’s best friend dig up his employers’ lawns. And as a journalist at the Boone County Gazette, he could not be trusted to dig up the dirt. After a series of pink-slips, Barry finally found his dream job...and he’s willing to fight for it.

However, one fearful night Barry notices those laid to rest have begun waking and fears it may be due to some mistake on his part. Barry must put the dead down again before the earnest folks in town notice anything is up. He may not be able to explain why the departed have returned as hobbling, green tinted monstrosities but he has a particular set of skills and he plans use them.

As Barry, fight zombies in this 2D procedurally-generated turn-based strategy game. Manage your to clean up the graveyard before the townspeople awake. Try not to get clawed to death while carefully managing your grave-plots. Just don’t forget to bury the bodies as only 6 feet of dirt can put the living dead down for good.

Team Gravedigger is a fresh independent games company formed by three students of Tufts University: Chad Myles, Kate Wasynczuk and David Taus. Gravedigger is the studio’s first offering and the first game developed by anyone on its crew. We hope you it!
